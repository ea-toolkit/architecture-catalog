# CRM Cloud Service

The core business service offered through the NovaCRM product. It bundles tenant management, subscription billing, and contact analytics into a single SaaS offering consumed by enterprise B2B customers.

## Service Scope

- **Tenant Management** — onboarding, provisioning, and lifecycle management
- **Subscription Billing** — plan management, invoicing, and usage tracking
- **Contact Analytics** — engagement metrics, usage dashboards, and reporting
