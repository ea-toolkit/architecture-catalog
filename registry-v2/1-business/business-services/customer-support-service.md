Customer Support Service handles all post-onboarding support needs for enterprise tenants. It covers technical troubleshooting, account configuration changes, user management assistance, and escalation workflows.

## Service Scope

- Technical issue triage and resolution
- Account configuration and plan changes
- User access and permission management support
- SLA monitoring and escalation handling
