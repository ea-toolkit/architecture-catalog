Customer Onboarding Service provides a guided onboarding experience for new enterprise customers. It handles workspace provisioning, SSO configuration, initial data imports, and administrator account setup.

## Service Scope

- Tenant workspace provisioning and configuration
- Single sign-on (SSO) and identity provider integration
- Bulk data import and migration assistance
- Welcome communications and training resources
