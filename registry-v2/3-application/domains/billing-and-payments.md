## Overview

Billing and Payments encompasses all subscription lifecycle management, payment processing, and revenue operations. This domain handles the full billing cycle from plan selection through recurring invoicing and payment reconciliation.

## Key Responsibilities

- Subscription plan management and pricing tiers
- Recurring billing cycles and invoice generation
- Payment processing and reconciliation
- Usage-based billing calculations
- Revenue reporting and financial analytics
