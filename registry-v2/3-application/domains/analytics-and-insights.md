## Overview

Analytics and Insights covers customer engagement analytics, usage reporting, and data-driven decision support. This domain aggregates data from across the platform to provide actionable business intelligence.

## Key Responsibilities

- Customer contact and engagement analytics
- Usage pattern analysis and threshold monitoring
- Data warehousing and query services
- Business intelligence and reporting dashboards
- Predictive analytics and trend forecasting
